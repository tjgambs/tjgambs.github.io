Quick Overview

Over Christmas break I decided I was going to take my Computer Science learning to a new level. Not only will I learn in the classroom, but I will also learn outside of the classroom, on my own. I decided to create Yahtzee. At the time I decided to take on this daunting task I had no idea how I was going to pull it off. I was still new to the language but I took it one line at a time and I was able to complete the project in a few days. I learned so much within the few days of writing the program and it was a great experience.
