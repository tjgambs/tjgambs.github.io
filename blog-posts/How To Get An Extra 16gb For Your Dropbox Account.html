How To Get An Extra 16gb For Your Dropbox Account

January 19, 2015
  • Requirements:
    A Dropbox Account
    Parallels Desktop 10 with an Operating System, I show how to get this for free here.

    1. On your primary desktop log into your dropbox account
    2. Once logged on, look at the top right hand corner of your web browser.
    3. Click on your name and then click on "Settings"
    4. Navigate to to the "Account Tab" then click on "invite your friends"
    5. If you are having trouble reaching this point click this
    6. Now copy the link that is to the right of "Copy Link"
    7. Keep this somewhere safe. Now onto Parallels.

    8. Open Parallels 10 and load up your preferred Operating System.
    9. Once the Operating System is loaded, go to your web browser.
    10. Now create an account with your referral link that you just copied.
    11. In order to save you the hassle of making 32 email addresses, go to this website website and use their pre-made email addresses.
    12. Once you create a dropbox account go to your email inbox and activate the account.

    Now check back to your main account, you should have 500mb more space. Onto the last part.

    13. Shut down your Parallels Desktop 10 Operating System.
    14. Now click on the settings option in your Parallels Desktop Control Center, it looks like a gear, to the right of the power button and to the left of the "install antivirus" buttons.
    15. Click "Network 1" on the left menu.
    16. Look for the MAC address option, then click generate.
    17. By generating a new MAC address, you are basically generating a new computer.

    18. Repeat steps 1-17 until your main account reaches 32 referrals, translating into 16gb of free space. Enjoy!
