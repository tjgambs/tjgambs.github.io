How To Get Parallels 10.0.1 For Free

January 19, 2015
  • 1. Click here to Download
    2. Install Parallels 10
    3. Disconnect Internet connection and force quit all instances of Parallels 10.
    4. Run Parallels 10
    5. On the Trial pop-up, click enter activation key OR click on Parallels Desktop in the menu bar at the top of the screen, then click "Activate Product..."
    6. Type any combo of letters and numbers, once you see a green check, press activate or continue.
    7. Once all activated and loaded, FORCE QUIT ALL INSTANCES OF PARALLELS 10.
    8. Open finder and click on your hard drive, mine is called Macintosh HD.
    9. Then click Library then Preferences then Parallels
    10. Look for a file called "licenses.xml" then delete it.
    11. Empty trash then Run Parallels 10, no trial window should pop up.
    12. Restart mac and run Parallels 10 again to ensure no trial boxes appear, if none do, you've successfully activated your copy.
    13. Make sure you never update the software otherwise you will have to pay for it.
